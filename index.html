About Me

I am inspired to paint in response to the wondrous West Wales landscape and coastline. I often paint and draw plein-air, producing watercolour notations within the landscape itself. I work in watercolours, oils, and acrylics, reflecting the beauty of the natural world and celebrating the sublime.
